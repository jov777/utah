OUR PRODUCTS

Pure Cycle by Pratt & Whitney

1. Evaporator
The evaporator, an ASME BP&V-coded vessel, is a shell-and-tube heat exchanger that utilizes carbon steel tubes that capture the resource heat. Optional materials, described in the Equipement list section, are available to meet site-specific needs. the evaporator is delivered as an integral part of the skid-module, thus reducing installation cost and site-specific ingineering.

2.Turbine-Generator Assembly
The heart of the PureCycle power systems is the turbine-generator assembly. This assembly is the same platform as Carrier's standard chiller motor-compressor; however it is operated in reverse as a turbine generator. High pressure vapor drives the turbine that in turn drives the induction generator through a gear transmission.

Pure Cycle by Pratt & Whitney

The oil and generator-cooling systems are integral to thr fully enclosed hermetic assembly as are the controls and diagnostics instrumentation for pressure, temperature, and speed monitoring. The turbine drives a two pole induction generator that transmits three-phase AC power at 480v/60Hz (or at 380/400/415v/50Hz with the alternative voltage option).


3. Condenser
The condenser, an ASME BP&-coded vessel, is a shell and tube heat exchanger that utilizes copper tubes in which lowpressure vapor is cooled and condensed into a saturated liquid. Optional materials, described in the Equipmentlist section, are available to meet site specific needs.

Pure Cycle by Pratt & Whitney

After the sale of Pratt & Whitney Power Systems by United Technologies to Mitsubishi, there still exisited a significant interest and market for these well- engineered, American manufactured ORC systems. Utah Power Systems recognized this need for leading renewable energy products and purchased a significant quantity of PureCycle 280kW systems. All units are thoroughly tested and calibrated and offered with full service maintenance and warranties. These units are skid mounted and ideal for smaller applications or in tandem for larger facilites. We provide full engineering and installation, as well as service and financing through CP Capital.
