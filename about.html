SGSP Solar Ponds:

Good Earth Mechanics (GEM) is the innovator of Salinity Gradient Solar Ponds. These specially designed ponds capture the energy of the sun in a high salt concentration brine, producing temperatures to over 200 degrees. This heat is kept in place by a gradient layer topped by a low salinity layer of nearly fresh water. Heat can be stored for up to 42 days in the absence of sunlight and is always availble to power ORC units. Over 10MW of electricity can power over 9,000 homes with clean renewable electricity from a one square mile pond site.

Renewable Energy Solutions for Waste Heat Recovery

Geothermal resources are undergound or at the surface in many areas worldwide. These geothermal resources provide sufficient heat to power ORC systems and provide large quantities of electricity. Many sites produce 50-150 megawatts or more in ORC-generated electricity. With the innovations of Good Earth Mechanics, salinity gradient solar ponds utilize high salt concentrations of brine to provide power. This forms solar batteries in lakes that power ORC systems and produce clean electricity. A six acre salinity gradient pond can produce up to 300kw of continuous power.
