Q: What is ORC?
A: Organic Rankine Cycle or ORC is a Rankine cycle sytem using an organic fluid as the heat transfer fluid. The R245fa fluid has a much lower vaporization point and can be used with low temperature heat sources.
Q: What types of heat sources can be utilized?
A: We can utilize most heat sources from industrial processes such as cement manufacturing, power plants, municipal waste incinerators, pulp mills etc. We can also utilize geothermal sources as well as solar heat.
What is a PPA?
A: PPA or purchase power agreement is an agreement by a power provider or distributor or large power user. guaranteeing to buy electricity output on a set price per kwh.
